Plug, Voltage & Time Zone
Power — Italy uses Type C, F and L plugs (2 round pins), 230V / 50Hz. Australian appliances (230-240V, 50Hz) generally work fine with just a plug adapter — a separate voltage converter usually isn't needed.
Time Zone — Italy is on CEST (UTC+2) during your trip. Australia doesn't observe daylight saving in May–June, so standard time zones apply: Italy is about 8 hours behind Sydney/Melbourne/Brisbane (AEST) and about 6 hours behind Perth (AWST) for the whole trip.
